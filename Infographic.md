# SOCIETAL ISSUES THAT CAN BENEFIT FROM CIVIC ENGAGEMENT

## CIVIL ENGAGEMENT
Involves people coming together to help improve the overall quality of life for everyone who lives and works in a
community through processes such as political activism, community service, and environmentalism.

## 1. HOMELESSNESS
Outreach specialists connect people that are experiencing homelessness to essential services like shelters,
food banks, and job training.

## 2. POLLUTION
Environmental scientists task with finding solutions and preventing pollution-related issues from developing.

## 3. CLIMATE CHANGE
Atmospheric scientists look into new and innovative ways to reverse the damage that climate change had caused
before it is too late.

## 4. FOOD INSECURITY
Food scientists develop new technologies and processes to increase food availability, reduce food waste, and
improve the nutritional value of food.

## 5. INEQUALITY
Urban planners develop land use plans and various programs to give everyone an equal chance at thriving despite
inequality issues.
